About These Resources

These resources provide immediate support and guidance for individuals dealing with drug addiction in South Africa. All services are free and confidential unless otherwise stated.

  • Department of Social Development Substance Abuse Line: Offers 24/7, free and confidential support by phone and text message at 081-444-7000.
  • Childline: Provides 24/7, free and confidential support over phone and online chat. They assist with various issues, including substance abuse.
  • Narcotics Anonymous (NA): A non-profit fellowship for recovering drug addicts who meet regularly to help each other stay clean.
  • Sanca: Offers prevention, treatment, and rehabilitation services for substance abuse.
  • South African National Council on Alcoholism and Drug Dependence (SANCA): A leading organization that offers prevention, treatment, and rehabilitation services.

Emergency

If this is a medical emergency, please call 10177 immediately. For immediate danger, call 10111 (SAPS).

Sources: Department of Social Development, Childline South Africa, Narcotics Anonymous South Africa, Sanca, SANCA National
