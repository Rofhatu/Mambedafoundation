About Our Foundation

Creating pathways from adversity to achievement

Our Mission

The Mambeda Foundation empowers individuals to unlock their potential through access to education, employment, addiction recovery, GBV prevention, small business development, and computer literacy in rural areas, while providing a foundation of care and support for orphaned children to build a brighter future.

Our Vision

A world where every person, regardless of background, has the guidance to succeed and where every orphaned child grows up in a supportive environment with equal opportunities, free from addiction, violence, and poverty.
