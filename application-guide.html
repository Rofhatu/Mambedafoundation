Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long should my CV be?

A: For most jobs, 2 pages is ideal. Recent graduates can use 1 page.

Q: When should I apply for university?

A: Applications typically open in March-April for the following year. Apply as early as possible.

Q: What if I don't meet all requirements?

A: Contact the institution directly. Some offer alternative pathways or recognition of prior learning.
